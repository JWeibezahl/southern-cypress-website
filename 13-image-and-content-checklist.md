# Image and Content Checklist

## Images Needed

### Branding

- Logo horizontal PNG
- Logo transparent PNG
- Favicon
- Social media profile image

### Website

- Hero image: kitchen with outdoor living visible
- Kitchen remodel image
- Bathroom remodel image
- Outdoor living image
- Deck or pergola image
- Exterior painting image
- Flooring and trim image
- Team or owner image
- Before and after images

## Photo Rules

Use warm natural light, clean finished work, real homes, detail shots, before and after comparisons, and exterior shots with Florida feel.

Avoid messy jobsite photos, low resolution images, dark photos, obviously fake mansion-only stock images, and overused generic contractor photos.

## Copy Rules

Use:

- renovations
- remodeling
- home improvements
- quality craftsmanship
- clear communication
- trusted trade partners when required
- attention to detail
- dependable service

Avoid:

- licensed GC claims
- structural construction
- roofing
- electrical
- plumbing
- HVAC
- ground-up construction
- all work in-house

## Conversion Elements

Every page should include:

- phone number
- quote CTA
- trust language
- service area
- photo or visual proof
- next step
