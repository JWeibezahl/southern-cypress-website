About Southern Cypress Homes

At Southern Cypress Homes, we believe your home should reflect the way you live: comfortable, functional, welcoming, and built with lasting quality in mind.

We are a Florida-based home renovation company focused on thoughtful residential improvements that elevate everyday living. From kitchen and bathroom remodels to outdoor living spaces, decks, painting, flooring, trim work, and full-home updates, we bring craftsmanship, communication, and attention to detail to every project we take on.

Our approach is simple. We show up when we say we will. We communicate clearly. We respect your home. We focus on quality work that we are proud to stand behind.

We understand that inviting someone into your home for a renovation project requires trust. That is why we focus on creating a smooth, professional experience from the first conversation to the final walkthrough.

Whether you are updating a single room or improving multiple areas of your home, our goal is to help you create a space that feels timeless, functional, and uniquely yours.
