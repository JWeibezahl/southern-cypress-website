Flooring & Trim

The details often make the biggest difference.

Flooring and trim work provide the foundation that ties a home's design together and creates a finished, cohesive appearance throughout the space.

Southern Cypress Homes helps homeowners update and enhance their interiors through quality flooring installations, trim upgrades, and finish carpentry that add character and value to the home.

From elegant baseboards and crown molding to durable flooring options that complement your lifestyle, we focus on craftsmanship that elevates the entire space.

Whether you're updating a single room or creating consistency throughout your home, quality flooring and trim work can completely transform the overall appearance and feel of your property.

These finishing touches are often what separate an ordinary room from one that feels truly complete.
