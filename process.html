Our Process

A Clearer, Smoother Renovation Experience.

Renovation projects should feel organized, professional, and well-communicated. Our process helps homeowners know what to expect from the first conversation to the final walkthrough.

1

Tell Us About Your Project

Call, text, or submit a quote request with details, photos, and your ideal timeline.

2

Schedule a Consultation

We review your goals, discuss options, and determine the best next step.

3

Review Your Estimate

You receive a clear estimate with scope details and coordination notes.

4

Approve the Plan

Once approved, we schedule the work, coordinate materials, and confirm timing.

5

Project Begins

Our team focuses on quality workmanship, respectful jobsite practices, and clear updates.

6

Final Walkthrough

We review completed work with you and make sure results feel complete.

Some projects require specialty licensed trades. When needed, Southern Cypress Homes works with trusted licensed trade partners to help complete the project properly.
