# HubSpot CRM Workflow

## Lead Sources

Track these sources:

- Website form
- Google Business Profile
- Google Local Services Ads
- Facebook
- Instagram
- Referral
- Yard sign
- Vehicle wrap
- Repeat customer
- Other

## Pipeline Stages

1. New Lead
2. Contacted
3. Consultation Scheduled
4. Estimate Needed
5. Estimate Sent
6. Follow-Up
7. Won
8. Lost
9. Not a Fit

## Required Lead Fields

- Name
- Phone
- Email
- Address
- Project Type
- Estimated Budget
- Timeline
- Lead Source
- Notes
- Photos Received
- Estimate Date
- Follow-Up Date

## Website Form Routing

When a website form is submitted:

- create contact
- create deal
- assign source: Website
- send notification email
- trigger follow-up task
- send confirmation email

## Confirmation Email

Subject:

> We Received Your Project Request

Body:

Thank you for contacting Southern Cypress Homes. We received your project request and will review the details as soon as possible.

If you have photos of the space, you can reply to this email or text them to us directly.

We look forward to learning more about your project.

Southern Cypress Homes  
Quality Craftsmanship. Homes Built to Last.

## Follow-Up Task

If no response after 24 hours:

"Follow up with website lead and ask if they would like to schedule a consultation."

## Missed Call Text

"Thanks for calling Southern Cypress Homes. We may be assisting another customer, but we will get back to you shortly. Feel free to text photos or project details here."
