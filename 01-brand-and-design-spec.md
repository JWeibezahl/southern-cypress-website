# Brand and Design Specification

## Brand Name

Southern Cypress Homes

## Domain

southerncypresshomes.com

## Tagline Options

Primary:

> Quality Craftsmanship. Homes Built to Last.

Secondary:

> Renovations That Elevate the Way You Live.

Alternative:

> Enhancing Homes. Elevating Lifestyles.

## Brand Personality

Southern Cypress Homes should feel warm, trustworthy, established, capable, Southern, high quality, practical, respectful, and professional.

It should not feel cheap, corporate, flashy, overly luxury, handyman-level, tactical, or generic contractor.

## Color Palette

Cypress Green: `#445B2A`  
Deep Forest Green: `#2E3B1F`  
Warm Gold: `#C8A25A`  
Soft Gold: `#D6B97A`  
Warm Cream: `#F4F1EA`  
Charcoal: `#2B2B2B`  
White: `#FFFFFF`

## Typography

Headings:

```css
font-family: "Cormorant Garamond", Georgia, serif;
```

Body, navigation, buttons:

```css
font-family: "Montserrat", Arial, sans-serif;
```

## Visual Style

Use warm home photography, clean cream backgrounds, dark green overlays, gold accents, serif headings, clear service cards, strong CTAs, and polished but approachable layouts.

Avoid harsh black backgrounds, bright yellow construction colors, cartoon contractor graphics, too many icons, fake luxury wording, and cluttered layouts.

## Image Direction

Use photography showing bright kitchens, clean bathrooms, outdoor living spaces, decks, exterior painting transformations, trim and finish details, warm Florida homes, and before and after project images.

Image mood should be natural light, warm tones, real homes, polished but attainable, not mansion-only.

## Buttons

Primary button: gold background, uppercase, strong letter spacing, squared or slight radius.

Secondary button: transparent with border, cream or green text depending on background.

## Mobile Design

Mobile requirements:

- sticky call button
- hamburger menu
- tap-to-call phone number
- contact form visible within two scrolls
- service cards stack cleanly
- images load fast
- no tiny text
