## Step 1: Tell Us About Your Project

Start by sharing what you want to improve. You can call, text, or submit a quote request with details, photos, and your ideal timeline.

## Step 2: Schedule a Consultation

We will review your project, ask questions, discuss your goals, and determine the best next step.

## Step 3: Review Your Estimate

You will receive a clear estimate based on the project scope. We explain what is included, what may require additional coordination, and what to expect.

## Step 4: Approve the Plan

Once the scope is approved, we schedule the work, coordinate materials, and communicate the plan.

## Step 5: Project Begins

Our team focuses on quality workmanship, respectful jobsite practices, and clear updates throughout the project.

## Step 6: Final Walkthrough

We review the completed work with you, answer questions, and make sure the final result feels complete.

## Trade Partner Language

Some projects require specialty licensed trades. When needed, Southern Cypress Homes works with trusted licensed trade partners to help complete the project properly.
